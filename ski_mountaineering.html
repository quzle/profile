Ski mountaineering

I love skiing, and more recently I have started ski mountaineering.

I started alpine skiing when my family brought me as a little boys, from the age of 4. In my teenage years I used to race (and generally, lose.. but had fun).

In the last few years I mostly lost interest in downhill skiing, partly because I find its impact on the environment to be unjustifyable at this time, and dislike the ethos of man 'conquering' and 'taming' a mountain by making it easily accessible. Additionally, I much prefer the satisfaction of reaching a summit through my own effort.

David, the climber
