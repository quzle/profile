Climbing

I love rock climbing.

I started climbing indoors at a bouldering gym in Geneva. I continued in Toulouse, then Coventry, Cardiff and finally Stoke-On-Trent. It was only after 5 years of indoor bouldering that I started climbing outdoors.

Some of my gym friends went bouldering outdoors, I started tagging along in 2022 and by the summer of 2023 I was spending most of my weekends in the Peak District or in Wales, bouldering. I also started rope climbing more, indoors and outdoors, and in 2024 was doing multi pitch routes in Wales and Devon, mainly with Jack and Dan, and in Italy with Jerome.
